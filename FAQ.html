General Questions

What is Super Gadget Store?

Super Gadget Store is a leading online retailer specializing in the latest electronic gadgets such as mobile accessories, computer&laptop accessories, gaming accessories and car gadgets. We offer a wide range of products from top brands at competitive prices, with a focus on customer satisfaction.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via email at support@supergadgetstore.com, by phone at +6012-123-1234, or through the live chat feature on our website. Our support hours are Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM (GMT+8).

Do you have physical stores?

We only operate exclusively as an online store currently.

Account & Shopping

How can I login to my account?

To login to your account, click on the "Login" button at the top right corner of our website. Enter your email address and password, then click "Sign In". If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link to reset it.

If you don't have an account yet, you can create one by clicking "Register" and filling out the required information. Having an account allows you to track orders, save your shopping preferences, and checkout faster.

How can I add items to my cart?

Adding items to your cart is simple:

  1. Browse our products and find an item you want to purchase
  2. Select the desired options (color, size, quantity, etc.) if available
  3. Click the "Add to Cart" button
  4. You'll see a confirmation message and the item will be added to your shopping cart

You can continue shopping or proceed to checkout by clicking the cart icon at the top of the page.

Ordering & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers. All payments are processed through secure encrypted channels to ensure your financial information is protected.

How do I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website and viewing your order history.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it by contacting our customer support team. After this period, your order may have already entered the processing stage and cannot be modified. Once shipped, orders cannot be canceled.

Shipping & Delivery

What are your shipping options?

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day
Shipping costs vary based on the option selected and your location.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times typically range from 7-14 business days, depending on the destination. Additional customs fees or import taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.

What should I do if my package is damaged or lost?

If your package arrives damaged or is lost in transit, please contact our customer support team within 48 hours of delivery (or expected delivery date for lost packages). We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue and arrange for a replacement or refund as appropriate.

Returns & Warranty

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be unused, in their original packaging, and include all accessories. Some items, such as software, personalized products, and hygiene-sensitive items, may not be eligible for return. Please see our full Returns Policy for details.

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return, log into your account and go to your order history. Select the item you wish to return and follow the prompts. Alternatively, you can contact our customer support team, and they will guide you through the process and provide you with a return authorization number.

Do your products come with a warranty?

Most of our products come with a manufacturer's warranty that typically ranges from 90 days to 2 years, depending on the product. The specific warranty details are provided on each product page. We also offer extended warranty options for many items at checkout.
